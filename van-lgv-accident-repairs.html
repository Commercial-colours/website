Van & LGV accident repairs

We cater for the lighter side of commercial vehicles, from car-derived vans up to large vans with long wheel-bases and high roofs. We repair minor dents and scrapes, carry out refurbishments, and heavy smash repairs.

Replacement vehicles

If you’re self-employed with only one vehicle, we understand its importance to your business. We will help with your accident claim and repair your vehicle quickly, sourcing a replacement if needed. For larger fleets, we handle all accident management needs nationwide.

End of contract

We offer end-of-lease de-fleeting services. If you have trucks or vans coming to the end of a contract or lease period, we can take care of de-decaling, cosmetic repairs and valeting to a very high standard.

To maintain your warranty, we are Mercedes Benz approved for both truck and van repairs. We have invested in specialist equipment and training to meet their exacting standards.
