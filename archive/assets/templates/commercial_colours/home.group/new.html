Commercial Vehicle Accident Repair & Truck Painting Centre

Commercial Colours, the leading Commercial Vehicle Accident Repair and Truck Painting centre covers the North West and North Wales from our base near Chester. Our facilities include Josam chassis alignment and straightening, Josam Cab alignment and repairs, Korek light commercial repair jigs, commercial vehicle spray booths and shot blasting.

Manufacturer approved repairs

We are leading CV crash repair specialists and approved repairers for Mercedes Benz UK and Scania Great Britain. Our staff also have extensive experience with DAF, Volvo, MAN and Iveco, as well as Ford, Renault and Vauxhall.

Insurance approved

We handle the repair process from start to finish. We are approved commercial vehicle repairers for Aviva, AXA, Royal & Sun Alliance and Zurich. LCVs and van accident repairs are catered for with an accident management service for larger fleets.

Accident management partners

We are approved commercial vehicle repairers for several well-established, national accident management networks including; Ai Claims, Nationwide, Sopp & Sopp, Countrywide Accident Assistance, WNS and Fleetwatch.

Van accident repairs
