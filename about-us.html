About Commercial Colours

We use the latest repair equipment and methods, and our staff have the experience and expertise to repair the most badly damaged commercial vehicles.

EPA registered

All our painting processes are carried out following strict environmental guidelines. We are EPA registered with the local authority and we use fully compliant products including water-based paints. Waste paint and thinners are recycled and re-used onsite.

Office of Fair Trading Approved

We became an independent company and moved to Deeside Industrial Park in 2005. We employ over twenty people and apprentices who we develop into qualified bodywork technicians. We were awarded VBRA membership, which also incorporates an Office of Fair Trading Approved Code.
